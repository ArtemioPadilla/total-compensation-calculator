How to Use

  • Add Offers: Click "+ Add Job Offer" to create a new offer card
  • Enter Details: Fill in all compensation components for each offer
  • Annual vs Monthly: Choose whether your salary is annual or monthly
  • Stock Value: Enter the total value of stock grants (RSUs, options, etc.)
  • Vesting Period: Specify how many years your stocks vest over
  • Bonuses: Include signing bonuses, annual performance bonuses, aguinaldo (Christmas bonus), and prima vacacional (vacation bonus)
  • PTO Days: Enter the number of paid time off days per year
  • Prima Vacacional: Enter the total amount directly, or calculate it from vacation days and percentage of daily salary
  • Medical Benefits: Enter the annual value of health, dental, and vision insurance
  • Other Benefits: Add monetary value of other benefits (401k match, gym, commuter, etc.)
  • Compare: The comparison table shows all offers side-by-side with the best values highlighted
  • Visualize: View interactive charts showing compensation over time, including stock vesting schedules, bonuses, and other time-based events
